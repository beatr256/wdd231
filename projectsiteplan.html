Individual Project Site Plan

Local Skateboarding Club Website

Site Name

Site Name: Local Skateboarding Club

This name was selected because it clearly represents a community-based skateboarding club that serves local skaters. The name is simple, descriptive, and easy to remember.

Optional domain availability: local-skateclub.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Local Skateboarding Club website is to provide information about the club, including upcoming skateboarding events, membership details, skate park locations, safety tips, and beginner tutorials. The site will also encourage new members to join and participate in community skateboarding activities.

Scenarios

  • How can I become a member of the Local Skateboarding Club?
  • Where and when are the next skateboarding events or meetups?
  • What skate parks are recommended for beginners?

Color Scheme

The following colors are used consistently throughout this site plan:

  • Deep Blue (#1f3c88): Used for headings, header, footer, and primary branding.
  • Warm Orange (#f4a261): Used for accents, borders, and highlights.
  • Light Gray (#f9f9f9): Used as the main background color.

Typography

  • Trebuchet MS: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to give a bold and modern look.
  • Arial / Helvetica: Used for body text for readability and accessibility.
